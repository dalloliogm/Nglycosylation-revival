## Notes

The manuscript should not be a close reproduction of the 2012 population-genetics analysis. That paper is historical context and a hypothesis generator. The central contribution should be the architecture hypothesis and a modern multi-evidence test.

The strongest current framing is: biological systems may localize evolvability away from catastrophic failure points. In N-glycosylation, upstream precursor synthesis, oligosaccharyltransferase activity, and ER quality-control machinery represent the robustness layer, while Golgi branching, terminal modification, and cell-surface glycan diversification represent the evolvable interface layer.
