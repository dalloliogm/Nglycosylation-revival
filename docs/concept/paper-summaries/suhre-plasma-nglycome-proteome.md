# Fine-Mapping of the Human Blood Plasma N-Glycome Onto Its Proteome

Citation key: `suhre_plasma_nglycome_proteome`

Authors: Suhre; et al.

Year: 2019

Journal: Metabolites

Link: https://doi.org/10.3390/metabo9060122

## Summary

This paper maps plasma N-glycome variation onto proteomic context. It helps connect bulk plasma glycan signals to specific glycoproteins and protein-level interpretation.

## Relevance To This Project

This is useful because one major risk of plasma glycome GWAS is that bulk glycan traits combine many proteins and tissues. Protein-resolved interpretation can help distinguish pathway-level glycan-output variation from changes driven by particular secreted proteins.

## Use In Manuscript

Use in methods and limitations for interpreting plasma N-glycome evidence.

Use as support for treating glycan-output data as informative but not automatically gene-specific.

## Limitations

The evidence remains plasma-biased and does not itself test N-glycosylation pathway-region architecture.
